3D-Graphic

Blender, Vectary & GitHub

This project was run by my intuition and the thrill of competing against other companies. The NHBRC was running a compaign between businesses to compete against each other where the goal was to help increase their brand awairness. the limitations where endless and I had full designer rights. The lego design was a personal request for the NHBRC, because they needed the design to look easy, welcoming, and familiar. There was a series of designs they needed to get done for each of their compaigns, but they all headto have a consistent look and feel to them. These designs was build really quickly as they requested and while our team was developing their new site, I was developing interactive 3D-Configurators to attach to their website.
3D designs like thses are extremely effective to increasing brand awareness and it certainly keeps users attracted to their websites in particular. Not only is this a 'fun' way to attract users and hold their attension, but it adds a proffesional touch to any organization!

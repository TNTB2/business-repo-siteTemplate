3D-Sculpting/Modeling

Blender

My passion lies in 3D-Sculpting/Modeling! therefore I make it high priotity to improve any and all skills I have regarding creating art and 3Dmaterials. 3D-Environments have endless creative capabilities and where the only limitation is, is the level of skill and imagination from an artist. I try my best when it comes to designing, and when I don't see a design work out, I find ways for pieces to fit together in a different approach or I start from scratch. these excersizes help me push my limits as well as learn different tecniques for future designs. These projects help me to understand processes better when designing products and are a great way to upskill my knowledge when it comes to working on deadlines! These models are normally retopologized and ready for animation when I'm done with them (that's if I didn't animate them already!). I mainly use blender to create models, because of its free access and its industry ready standards and compatibility with other platforms as well as render engines.
